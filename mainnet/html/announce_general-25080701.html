[ Release Notes ] v0.0.2 (2025.08.07)
1. Feature Updates & Changes
⦁ Fishing ∘ 🎣 A peaceful new activity has arrived. You can now enjoy fishing in PLAY3! ∘ Head over to the pond, river, or seaside and try casting a line. ∘ You might reel in something fun! ⦁ Map Construction ∘ 🗺️ Some gentle remodeling was done across the map. ∘ New fishing spots like rivers and coastlines have been prepared. ∘ We hope you enjoy the refreshed scenery as much as the new activity it brings!

⦁ Meet New NPC, Pawnbroker Lick! ∘ 💼 The owner of the mysterious Item Garage Box has finally arrived in the world of PLAY3! ∘ Lick is here to buy certain items from you, but only the ones he likes. ∘ Bring your extras and see what kind of deal you can strike. ∘ He might not pay top coin… but he does know his business. 😉 ⦁ Lick's Pawn Shop ∘ You can now sell multiple items to Lick at once. ∘ Selling items grants you points, and accumulating points lets you open Pawn Shop Mystery Boxes (Pawn Boxes). ∘ Only 3 Pawn Boxes can be opened each day, but Lick will continue buying your items even after the limit. ∘ Special items exclusive to Pawn Boxes have been added. ⦁ Missions ∘ New fishing-related missions have been added to both Event and Daily Missions. ∘ ‘Close your Bazaar’ mission has been removed from Daily Missions. ∘ Mission UI has been improved for better clarity and usability. ※ 'NEW' tag will appear when new missions are added. ⦁ Inventory ∘ An auto-sort feature has been added, along with an expanded visible area. ⦁ Tree Reset Time ∘ Tree cooldowns now reset together at 00:00 UTC every day.
